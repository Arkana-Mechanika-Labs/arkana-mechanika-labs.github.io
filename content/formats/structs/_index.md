Shared data structures used across multiple Darklands file formats. All offsets and field names are sourced from the canonical KB specifications (`structures.xml`, `dksaveXX.sav.xml`).

At the moment, the shared/common material is split across existing pages instead of a separate "Common" section:

- [Save File](/formats/save-file/) covers `date`, `date_reversed`, `money`, `coordinates`, and `person_colors`
- [Character Record](/formats/structs/character/) covers `attribute_set`, `skill_set`, and `item`
